Ocean Bridge - 1.21.7 (2025 july)

I love building bridges. This build is only part of a bigger one, meant to be an adventure map, but I'll probably never finish it. I tried white, because I wanted to use the pale oak. I am so not used to having white blocks(that have stairs and other variants), that I am only realizing at the time of writing, I could have used pale oak trapdoors instead of iron ones.
Inspired by fromsoft games, and Bdubs' Hermitcraft S10 nether hub.
Shaderpack: Sildur's Vibrant shaders

UTSMP2 Dark Bridge - 1.21 (2024 august)

The world download of the SMP is lost, so I can't actually go back and take screenshots of what it looked like on the server. But thankfully I was able to find it in my creative world, and place to into an all plains world, to mimic what it was like. It's probably the biggest thing I've ever built in survival. I mined all the blackstone myself, without shulker boxes and elytra btw. It felt amazing to show off my building skills to a whole a server, and a livestream audience. I also designed the flying machine that works as a fromsoft elevator.
It was inspired by Elden Ring's Divine Tower bridges, and how massive they look from below.
